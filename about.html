About Musikata

Mission

Musikata's mission is to help the world be more musical.

We believe that music is one of our greatest gifts, and we want to help more people get into music.

Why are we called 'Musikata'?

Our name is a combination of the words: Music and Kata.

Kata are sets of exercises which have been used by martial arts students for centuries.

They help students master fundamental skills through regular practice. And what's cool is that they really work!

We think that the idea of Kata can help people learn music. They are at the heart of our dojo.

What do we do?

In the martial arts, a dojo is a gathering place where students train. We are a musical dojo.

We provide fun exercises, courses, and other resources that help people learn music.

In addition we create space for a community of people who want to learn music together.

What makes us different?

We're focused on making the process of learning music skills fun, effective, and soulful.

  • Scientific

    We believe in scientific learning. What really helps people learn music and connect with each other? This is a question that fascinates us.

    We try to apply the latest research to help people learn more effectively.

    In addition, as our members learn music, we can study what works and what doesn't.

    So our members are also helping us discover how people actually learn music.

  • Core Concepts

    Musikata's methods are based on three main ideas:

    1. Divide and Conquer: You can learn anything if you can break it down into simpler pieces.
    2. Daily Delight: Learning is fun and effective when you do it for at least little bit every day.
    3. Community: You learn better when you do it with other people.
