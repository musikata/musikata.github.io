About Musikata

Mission

Musikata's mission is to help the world be more musical.

We believe that music is one of our greatest gifts, and we want to help more people get into music.

What do we do?

We provide exercises, courses, and other resources that help people learn music.

In addition we create space for a community of people who want to learn music together.

What makes us different?

  • Core Concepts

    Musikata's methods are based on three main ideas:

    1. Divide and Conquer: You can learn anything if can figure out how to break down into simpler pieces.
    2. Daily Delight: Learning becomes fun and effective if you do it just for at least a little bit every day.
    3. Community: Learning is even better when you do it together. It's community that helps us stick with it and enjoy it.

    Numerous studies have shown that learning based around these ideas is more effective and enjoyable.

  • Beauty and Soul

    We believe that learning music should be beautiful and soulful. So, we do our best to make our dojo reflect this through our imagery and our community.

  • Scientific

    We also believe in scientific learning. What things really help people learn music and connect with each other? How can we help each other learn more effectively? These are the questions that fascinate us.

    As our members learn music, they are also helping us discover how people actually learn music.

  • Fun

    And of course we think learning about music should be fun! So we try to make our resources like delightful challenges.
